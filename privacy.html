2. Scope of This Policy

This Policy applies to:

  • Vendors who create accounts and upload data.
  • Buyers (who remain anonymous and do not need accounts) who access public pages and tracking links.
  • All personal data processed by us through the Platform.

It does not cover:

  • Any transactions, chats, payments, or personal data exchanged privately on WhatsApp between buyers and vendors.
  • Third-party websites or services linked from the Platform.

3. Personal Data We Collect

From Vendors (Data Subjects with Accounts):

  • Account information: full name, email, phone number, password (hashed).
  • Vendor profile: business name, slug (e.g., “glamstore”), bio, Lagos area, WhatsApp number, avatar.
  • Product data: titles, descriptions, prices, variants (color, size, etc.), stock levels, uploaded images.
  • Order data you initiate: order numbers, snapshots of selected products/variants, total amounts, expected delivery dates, rider phone/name (when added), status updates.
  • Usage data: login history, page views, analytics.

From Buyers (Anonymous):

  • No account is created. We may temporarily log technical data such as IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited (for security, analytics, and to improve the Platform).
  • When a buyer submits a review after marking an order “Received”, we collect the rating, comment, and any optional photo — tied only to the order ID (no buyer name or contact is required or stored).

Automatically Collected (All Users):

  • Cookies and similar technologies (for functionality, security, and basic analytics — see Section 10).
  • Server logs: IP address, access times, referring URLs.

We practice data minimization — we collect only what is necessary for the purposes described below.

4. Lawful Basis and Purposes of Processing

We process personal data based on the following lawful bases under the NDPA (Section 25):

  • Performance of a contract — to provide you with the Platform services (e.g., vendor account management, product hosting, order tracking links).
  • Legitimate interests — to operate, secure, and improve the Platform, prevent fraud, and analyze usage (where these interests do not override your rights).
  • Consent — where we require it (e.g., for marketing communications or certain non-essential cookies). You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legal obligation — to comply with Nigerian laws, NDPC requirements, court orders, or tax/regulatory duties.

Specific Purposes:

  • To create and manage vendor accounts and storefronts.
  • To host and display product catalogs and generate shareable links.
  • To enable public order tracking and status updates.
  • To collect and display genuine reviews for trust-building.
  • To improve the Platform through analytics.
  • To respond to support requests or legal inquiries.
  • To prevent abuse, fraud, or violations of our Terms.

We do not use personal data for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

5. How We Share Personal Data

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers (data processors) such as Supabase (hosting/database), Vercel (deployment), and analytics tools — all bound by data processing agreements and NDPA-compliant.
  • Legal authorities — if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights/safety.
  • In business transfers — if theosnnetwork is acquired or merged (with notice where feasible).

Vendors’ WhatsApp numbers and product information are publicly visible on their storefronts because that is the core purpose of the Platform. Buyers see only what vendors choose to share via public links.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined or as required by law:

  • Vendor account data: while the account is active + up to 7 years after deletion (for legal/tax reasons).
  • Product and order data: as long as needed for tracking and reviews, then archived or deleted.
  • Reviews: retained publicly on the vendor profile unless removal is requested and justified.
  • Technical logs: up to 90 days (or longer for security investigations).

When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data, including encryption (in transit and at rest where possible), access controls, regular security reviews, and secure hosting on Supabase and Vercel. However, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your vendor account credentials confidential.

8. Your Rights as a Data Subject (NDPA)

You have the following rights (subject to applicable exceptions):

  • Right to be informed — about how we process your data (this Policy).
  • Right of access — to request a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to rectification — to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure (“right to be forgotten”) — to request deletion (subject to legal retention obligations).
  • Right to restrict processing.
  • Right to object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to data portability (where technically feasible).
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time (where consent is the basis).
  • Right to complain to the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC).

To exercise any right, email us at message@dashboard.myvendor.qzz.io with a clear description and proof of identity. We will respond within 30 days (or as required by NDPA).
