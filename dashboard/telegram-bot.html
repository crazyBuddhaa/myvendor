Receive order alerts and query your store from Telegram — 24/7, no dashboard needed
What the bot does

Message your bot on Telegram and get instant live data from your store. When a customer checks out, you get a Telegram notification instantly.

/start→ Link your Telegram to your store
orders→ Today's count and pending order list
revenue→ This month's earnings
products→ Inventory count and out-of-stock alert
stats→ Full store overview in one message
Any sentence→ AI answers in plain English (requires Anthropic key)

Full setup guide (~5 min)
1
Create your bot with BotFather
Open Telegram → search @BotFather → send /newbot
Choose a name (e.g. MyStore Assistant) and a username ending in bot (e.g. mystorebot).
BotFather will give you a bot token — copy it.
2
Add environment variables to Vercel
Vercel → your project → Settings → Environment Variables:
TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN = token from BotFather (e.g. 7123456789:AAF…)
TELEGRAM_BOT_USERNAME = username you chose (without @, e.g. mystorebot)
ANTHROPIC_API_KEY = from console.anthropic.com (optional — enables AI replies)
After adding vars, click Redeploy in Vercel so they take effect.
3
Update the bot username in constants.js
In your repo, open assets/js/constants.js and change:
TELEGRAM_BOT_USERNAME = 'your-actual-bot-username'
This keeps the Settings page "Link Telegram Bot" button pointing to the right bot.
4
Register the webhook
Use the form above (Step 1) to register the webhook automatically, or run this curl command manually:
curl -X POST "https://api.telegram.org/botYOUR_TOKEN/setWebhook" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"url":"https://myvendor.qzz.io/telegram"}'
5
Link your Telegram account to your store
Go to Settings → Notification Bot → select Telegram → tap Link Telegram Bot.
Tap Start in the bot chat that opens. You'll get a confirmation message.
6
Test it!
Message your bot with "stats" — it should reply with your live store overview.
Then go to your storefront, add a product to cart, and check out — you should receive a Telegram order notification within seconds.
Note: Make sure SUPABASE_URL and SUPABASE_SERVICE_ROLE_KEY are already set in Vercel (from your initial setup). The Telegram bot uses those to look up your store data.
