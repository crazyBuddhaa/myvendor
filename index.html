Simple Process

No tech skills needed. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use myvendor.

Features

Everything your buyers need,
nothing they don't

Smart WhatsApp Button

When a buyer picks a size, color, and quantity — the Order button auto-fills a WhatsApp message with all the details. No typing mistakes, no confusion.

Public Order Tracking

You create the order from your dashboard. Buyers get a tracking link like /track/MV-250325-001 — no login, no app, just open and see the status.

Beautiful Product Catalogs

Upload multiple photos, show all variants, and let buyers swipe through — like an Instagram shop but faster and with less data usage.

Verified Reviews

After delivery, buyers leave a review right from the tracking page. Ratings are tied to real orders, so your 4.9 stars actually mean something.

Zero Buyer Sign-ups

Your customers never create an account or share their data with us. They just browse, order on WhatsApp, and track. Fast and frictionless.

Shareable Clean Links

Every product has its own link. Post it on WhatsApp Status, in groups, or with your product photos. Links are short, clean, and work offline when loaded.
